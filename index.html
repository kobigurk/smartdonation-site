Empowering Non-Profit Organizations

At the Smart Donation Foundation we believe that non-profit organizations can operate more efficiently. By utilizing financial methods, data aggregation and transparency, these organizations can be smarter with their spending patterns and prove their credibility and reponsibility to donators.

Non-Profit Organizations

Easy-to-use tools to monitor, control and manage donation money.

Transparency

Reduce corruption and keep donators informed of the use of their donation money by tying every expense to a public record.

Financial Responbility

Utilizing financial methods and advice to assist the organization in operating in a smart manner in their respective ecosystems.

Data Aggregation

Collecting data from muliple organizations to construct a common ground of operations.

Blockchain

We use the blockchain technology to provide immutable and traceable records for every financial transaction happens inside or outside the organization. We use multi-signature to make sure every expense is reasonable or approved by a financial expert.

Open-Source

Discussion and development are transparently done in public using open-source tools and platforms - everyone who's interested can observe the proejct, join the effort and contribute code. Have a look at our GitHub repository.
